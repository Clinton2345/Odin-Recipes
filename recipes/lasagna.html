Lasagna

HOME

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with a rich meat sauce, a creamy cheese mixture and plenty of melted mozzarella. Each layer is tucked to create heartly casserole that's golden buubly when baked.

Ingredients

For the meat sauce:

  • 2 table spoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500 grams ground beef or sausage
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 table spoons tomatoe paste
  • 1 tea spoon dried oregano
  • salt and pepper

For the cheese mixture:

  • 1.5 cups riccota cheese
  • An egg
  • 0.5 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

For layering:

  • 9 =lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shradded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the meat sauce
  2. Mix cheese filling
  3. Cook noodles
  4. Layer in a baking dish
  5. Bake at 180 degrees celsius
  6. Let it rest for 10 mins then serve
