Decription

This chicken features tender, juicy chicken breasts or thighs, seared to a golden perfection, then smeared in a rich garlic butter sauce with herbs. It's simple yet packed with flavor, perfect for a weeknight dinner or cozy weekend meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or thighs)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 2 table spoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • One tea spoon dried thyme
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper
  2. Sear the chicken in olive oil over medium-high heat for abou 4-5 minuites per side, until golden and cooked through. Remove from pan and set aside.
  3. Mend the butter in the same pan. Add minced garlic and thyme, fry fragrant.
  4. Degkaze the pan with broth or wine, sraping up brown bit from the bottom
  5. Return the chicken to the pan, spoonong the sauce over it. Simmer for 2-3 minuite.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve worm.
